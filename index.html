ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Z, and I’m a 19 year-old makeup artist with over four years of experience. Makeup has been a passion of mine for as long as I can remember, and art has always been at the core of who I am. That love for creativity goes beyond makeup, which is why you’ll see a few other crafted and artistic elements throughout this site. They represent my personality, my vision, and the world behind BLACKKULTX. I’m excited to share that with you and with everyone who connects to my work.

PREPARATION TIPS

01

Wash & Dry your face, do not moisturize

02

Make sure your face is shaved to your liking for better coverage

03

Have your inspo picture ready /have an idea of what you want before hand

04

It’s best to have your hair already done its your choice!

05

Make sure you know your skin type ( is it oily, dry, or both) let me know when I arrive

06

Wear a zipup or button up so u can easily switch into clothes of your choice after the appointment.
